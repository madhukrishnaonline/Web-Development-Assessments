Social Media Overview

Facebook

Facebook is a social networking platform that allows users to connect, share, and communicate with friends and family. It's used by over 2.8 billion people worldwide.

Twitter

Twitter is a microblogging platform that enables users to post and interact with short messages called "tweets." It’s popular for real-time news and updates.

Instagram

Instagram is a photo and video-sharing social network. It allows users to share moments with followers and is known for its filters and visual-centric features.

LinkedIn

LinkedIn is a professional networking site that connects job seekers, employees, and employers. It’s a valuable resource for career development and professional networking.
